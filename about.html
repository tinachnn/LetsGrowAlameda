Who We Are

Let’s Grow! Alameda began as a means to solve various mental health problems that arose from the current public health crisis (COVID-19), including stress, anxiety, and depression. Young people bear a disproportionate burden of these mental health difficulties, and gardening has long been considered therapeutic for those struggling with mental health. As students ourselves, we recognize that quarantine presents a unique opportunity for young children to engage with the environment and science very intimately. We hope for young people throughout the greater Bay Area to begin gardening and learn its value through their interactions with plants and animals, alike.
